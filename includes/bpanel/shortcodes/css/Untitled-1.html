[tabs style="1"] [tab title="About the Shortcode"]

This shortcode let's you easily create tabs within your pages. There are 5 different styles of tabs including a vertical tab. The shortcode is made out of 2 shortcodes. One wrapping shortcode "tabs" and children shortcodes "tab".

[padding size="10"]

Usage:

[plain][[tabs style="1"]] [[tab title="Tab Title"]] ... [\tab] [[tab title="Tab #2"]] ... [\tab] [[tab title="Third Tab"]] ... [\tab] [\tabs][/plain] [/tab] [tab title="Attributes"]

These are the attributes you can use:

[column size="one-half"] [header style="fancy1" type="h6"]tabs Shortcode[/header]
  • style: the style of your tabs. There are 4 styles and a vertical style as well.
    Options: 1, 2, 3, 4, vertical â€” Default: 1
[/column] [column size="one-half" last="last"] [header style="fancy1" type="h6"]tab Shortcode[/header]
  • title: Title of your tab
    Default: Title
[/column] [/tab] [tab title="Just another tab"]
